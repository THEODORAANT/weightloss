We’re pleased to introduce our GetWeightLoss Refer a Friend Scheme, designed to reward you for recommending our programme to people you trust.

If you’re enjoying your journey with GetWeightLoss, why not share it? When someone you refer joins the programme and completes their first payment, you’ll receive a £5 discount off your own programme payments.

How it works:

• You must be an active GetWeightLoss patient.
• Share your unique referral ID with a friend, family member, or personal contact.
• Your referral must be a new patient, aged 18+ and based in the UK.
• Once they’re accepted and complete their first programme payment, your discount is applied automatically.

There’s no limit to how many people you can refer. Each successful referral earns you a £5 discount, which will be applied to future payment cycles until fully used.

This scheme is our way of saying thank you for spreading the word and helping others access safe, clinician-led weight loss treatment.
